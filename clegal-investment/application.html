Application Requirements for a Mortgage Loan

Elegibility Requirements

Dependents:

1)Copy of both sides of passport - 6 pay stubs.
2)Proof of paid sick leave (if applicable).
3) Copy of last employment contract or seniority certificate.
4)Certificate of CMF debt Payment slip of the credits you have (TDC among others)
where we can see quota in which you are paying and detail of the amount of quota.

Independent

1)Copy of identity card on both sides.
2)Certificate of commencement of activities
3)Folder in form 29 (F29) of the last two years.
4)Summary of receipts or invoices, last 2 years.
5)Last 8 bills or invoices separately.
6)Certificate of CMF debts
7)Payment card of the credits that it possesses (TDC among others), where we can
see quota in which it goes of payment and detail of the amount of quota.

Legal Entities

1)Certificate of commencement of activities.
2)Form 29 (F29) file for the last two years.
3)Tax balance for the last 2 years.
4)Summary of receipts or invoices for the last 2 years.
5)Last 8 bills or invoices separately.
6)Certificate of CMF debts
7)Payment card of the credits that you have (TDC among others) where we
can see the quota in which you are paying and detail of the amount of the quota.
