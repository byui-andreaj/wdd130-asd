Application Requirements for a Mortgage Loan

Elegibility Requirements

Dependents:

*Copy of both sides of passport - 6 pay stubs. *6 pay stubs. *Proof of paid sick leave (if applicable). * Copy of last employment contract or seniority certificate. *Certificate of CMF debt Payment slip of the credits you have (TDC among others) where we can see *quota in which you are paying and detail of the amount of quota.

Independent

*Copy of identity card on both sides. *Certificate of commencement of activities *Folder in form 29 (F29) of the last two years. *Summary of receipts or invoices, last 2 years. *Last 8 bills or invoices separately. *Certificate of CMF debts *Payment card of the credits that it possesses (TDC among others), where we can see quota in *which it goes of payment and detail of the amount of quota.

Legal Entities

*Certificate of commencement of activities. *Form 29 (F29) file for the last two years. *Tax balance for the last 2 years. *Summary of receipts or invoices for the last 2 years. *Last 8 bills or invoices separately. *Certificate of CMF debts *Payment card of the credits that you have (TDC among others) where we can see the quota in which you are paying and detail of the amount of the quota.
