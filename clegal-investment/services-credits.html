Types of Credits

Leasing Habitacional

Financing that includes a subsidy as well as a credit amount that is based on your income.

Conditions

*To be the first owner. *Not to buy from a relative within the fourth degree of consanguinity. *Credit quota from 900UF to 2000UF. *Minimum income $ 550,000. *Duration of the credit 20 years if or if.

BENEFITS:

*Automatic subsidy. *It is not mandatory and in 90% of the cases it is 5%. *Debt is not displayed. *Financing at 100% .

Mortgage Loan

Financing, that covers up to 80% of the value of the property, does not include subsidy, it is a linear credit.

Conditions

*Minimum income $900.000 *For individuals and legal entities. *Preferably professionals. *Foot accreditation.

BENEFITS

*Debt is not displayed. *In some cases only the foot credit is required. *Financing option from 8 years. *Low interest rate.
