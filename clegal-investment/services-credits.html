Types of Credits

Leasing Habitacional

Financing that includes a subsidy as well as a credit amount that is based on your income.

CONDITIONS

1)To be the first owner.
2)Not to buy from a relative within the fourth degree of consanguinity.
3)Credit quota from 900UF to 2000UF.
4)Minimum income $ 550,000.
5)Duration of the credit 20 years if or if.

BENEFITS:

1)Automatic subsidy.
2)It is not mandatory and in 90% of the cases it is 5%.
3)Debt is not displayed.
4)Financing at 100% .

Mortgage Loan

Financing, that covers up to 80% of the value of the property, does not include subsidy,
it is a linear credit.

CONDITIONS

1)Minimum income $900.000
2)For individuals and legal entities.
3)Preferably professionals.
4)Foot accreditation.

BENEFITS

1)Debt is not displayed.
2)In some cases only the foot credit is required.
3)Financing option from 8 years.
4)Low interest rate.
