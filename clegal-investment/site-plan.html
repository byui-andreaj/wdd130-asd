About us and our activities in C Legal Investment


We are an agency dedicated to providing you with specialized advice for the management of mortgage loans, we are in charge of guiding you to achieve the best financing opportunity, representing you legally and administratively throughout the process until you get the key to your property.


Our main objective is that you can have the peace of mind and security that when you get the CREDIT APPROVAL, you will not have to waste time performing cumbersome processes and that you may not know to deed, throughout the process we will be representing you.

Services and Credits

Types of Credits


Leasing Habitacional :Financing that includes a subsidy as well as a credit amount that is based on your income.
BENEFITS :
Automatic subsidy.
It is not mandatory and in 90% of the cases it is 5%.
Debt is not displayed.

Mortgage Loan: Financing, that covers up to 80% of the value of the property, does not include subsidy, it is a linear credit.
BENEFITS:
Debt is not displayed.
In some cases only the foot credit is required.
Financing option from 8 years.
Low interest rate.
